Process of Making Teddy Bear

    Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902 September 9 is Teddy Bear Day The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff. She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.

Process of Making Cicada

    Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans. Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism. Their lengthy life cycles may help them evade predators The cicadas are a superfamily, the Cicadoidea, of insects in the order Hemiptera. They are in the suborder Auchenorrhyncha, along with smaller jumping bugs such as leafhoppers and froghoppers.
